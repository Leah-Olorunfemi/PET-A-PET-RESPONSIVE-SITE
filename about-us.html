PET - A - PET

Welcome to PET-A-PET, a non-profit organization dedicated to promoting the welfare and well-being of pets. Our VISION is a world where pets rights matter and pet cruelty is non-existent. We are focused on ensuring that pets get adequate food, water, shelter, and medical care, as well as ensuring that they are free from unnecessary pain, suffering, and distress. At PET-A-PET we believe that every pet deserves a loving and safe home, and we work tirelessly to ensure that animals in our community receive the care and attention they deserve. Since our founding in 1998, we have helped thousands of pets find their forever homes, and we continue to provide critical services and support to animals and their owners every day. PET-A-PET advocates for the humane treatment of pets and opposes any form of cruelty or exploitation. It recognizes that animals have intrinsic value and that their welfare should be respected and protected. Overall, we seek to promote the physical, emotional, and mental health of animals and to minimize their suffering.

Our team of experienced and dedicated staff and volunteers are passionate about pet welfare, and we are committed to making a positive impact in the lives of pets and their owners. Whether you are interested in adopting a pet, volunteering your time, or making a donation to support our efforts, we welcome you to join us in our mission to promote the welfare and well-being of pets in our community.

OUR HISTORY

PET-A-PET was established in 1998 by a group of passionate animal lovers who recognized the need for a dedicated organization to promote the welfare and well-being of pets. At the time, there were few resources available to support pet owners, and many animals were in need of medical care, shelter, and support. From our early days, we have been commmitted to making a positive difference in the lives of pets and their owners. We started with a small group of volunteers who worked tirelessly to provide shelter, medical care, and support to animals in need. Over time, the organization has grown and expanded its programs and services to better serve pets. In the early days, we faced a lot of challenges and obstacles, but our organization's dedicated staff and volunteers remained solidly committed to their mission. They worked tirelessly to build partnerships with other organizations, raised awareness about responsible pet ownership, and advocated for the needs of pets in our community. Today, PET-A-PET is a respected and well-known organization, and we continue to be guided by the same passion and commitment that drove our founding founders in 1998. We are proud of the impact that we have made in the lives of pets and their owners over the years, and we remain dedicated to promoting the welfare and well-being of all pets in our community.

OUR TEAM

At PET-A-PET, our team members are the heart and soul of our organization and we're extremely fortunate to have a dedicated and passionate group of staff and volunteers who are committed to promoting the welfare and well-being of pets in our community. Our staff members bring a wealth of experience and expertise to their roles, and they are committed to providing the best possible care and support to the animals in our care. They work tirelessly to ensure that our programs and services run smoothly, and that the animals in our care receive the medical care, shelter, and support they need. In addition to our staff members, we are also fortunate to have a large and dedicated group of volunteers who support our organization in countless ways. Our volunteers assist with everything from animal care to fundraising to community outreach, and they play a vital role in helping us achieve our mission. We are grateful for the dedication and commitment of our team members, and we recognize that our organization would not be able to accomplish all that we do without their hard work and support. Whether you are interested in joining our team as a staff member or a volunteer, we welcome you to join us in our mission to promote the welfare and well-being of pets in our community.
